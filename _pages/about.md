I am a theoretical high-energy physicist with a PhD from NC State and postdoctoral experience at UConn. My research background is in QCD at small Bjorken-x — the Color Glass Condensate, JIMWLK/BK evolution, and dipole phenomenology.

I am interested in understanding deep learning systems through the lens of statistical mechanics and field theory, and in applying ML to problems in non-perturbative QCD. I am looking for research engineering and reinforcement learning positions at frontier AI labs.
